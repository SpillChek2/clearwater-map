The Nor-Oriente Amazon

Over three decades of oil drilling in the Ecuadorian Amazon, Chevron dumped more than 18 billion gallons of toxic wastewater into the rainforest, leaving local people suffering a wave of cancers, miscarriages and birth defects. ClearWater is partnering with five indigenous tribes — Cofan, Siona, Secoya, Quichua and Huaorani — to address the immediate environmental and health crisis.
